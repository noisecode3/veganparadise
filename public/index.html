Vegan BBQ hamburger with Mangoraja

With Anamma's formable mince it is easier than ever to become master chef in the green kitchen

Mix the vegan mince with BBQ sauce and then form into vegan hamburgers. Fry on the stove or grill outdoors. Add a slice of cheese for the last few minutes and let melt. While the burgers are cooking, you quickly mix up a tasty Mangoraja. Finally, just serve the burgers in hamburger buns together with the sauce and optional vegetables!
